TINKERSTOP – Think, Learn and Grow!




“To do is to learn”, as that is when the brain’s creative juices are put to test. Did you ever have the curiosity to know how your TV remote works? Or your microwave oven? Or even your fancy looking smart watches? Constructive destruction is always the ideal way to learn about the working of any given thing – be it the simplest toy car or even the 3D printer.


Makerspace, a common place where all kinds of people – professionals or amateurs, can create working models they are passionate about in an ecosystem that nurtures learning by exploring. It is all about getting hands-on and creating real-world projects using the resources and tools available.


TinkerStop is one such place which allows you to think without any bounds, learn whatever can be learnt and grow in any given direction. Mentors, tools, and a community of learners is what makes TinkerStop a working space to be at.


The next question that may strike your mind would be as to why you should be coming here. For which, the answer comes down to two things:


(1) The need for creative thinkers, and
(2) Development in problem solving skills.


In today's fast moving world where everything is a variable, technology is running at a fast pace and for us to be on par with it, it becomes essential to be practical and smart.


TinkerStop thrives in achieving that for each individual who steps into its space.


When it comes to engineering students, studies have shown that students experience deep learning when they learn by doing. This makerspace turns every student into a creator, allowing them to find connections between the separate functions like math, science, language, and art to create a full picture.
